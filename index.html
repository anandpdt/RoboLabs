About Our Lab

Introduction

RoboLabs is a structured technical ecosystem designed by IIT Patna student to foster creativity and mechanical innovation. We aim to transform ideas into physical prototypes through rigorous, hands-on learning in robotics and automation.

What We Do?

We design, build, and program autonomous systems. Our labs host intensive offline workshops that bridge the gap between textbook physics and hardware reality. Students master electronics, embedded C++, and mechanical design in a collaborative, elite engineering environment.
